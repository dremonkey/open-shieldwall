Theme Modifications

The final step is to make a few modification to your theme in order to hide your prices. This isn't difficult to do, but it will require you to change multiple theme template files and you should have some familiarity with html if you plan on doing this yourself.

Please remember that because each theme is different there is no way to be 100% sure that your prices are hidden without examining your theme code. The steps here will cover the majority of themes. Please contact myself or another Shopify expert if you want to be 100% sure your prices are hidden.

Search your theme for product.price or variant.price and wrap every occurence with a {% if customer.tags contains 'paywall-sub' %} conditional.
